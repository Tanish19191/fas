Sample Submission Guide

Follow our guidelines to ensure accurate and timely analysis of your agricultural samples

How to Submit Samples

Simple steps for proper sample collection and submission

1. Prepare Your Samples

Ensure samples are properly collected, dried, and stored in clean, appropriate containers. For forages, collect representative samples from multiple locations in the field or storage.

  • Use clean, dry containers to prevent contamination
  • Label each sample clearly with permanent marker
  • Submit at least 500g of material for accurate analysis
  • Keep samples cool and dry until shipping

2. Complete the Form

Download and fill out the Sample Submittal Form completely. Include all required information for accurate processing and billing.

  • Provide complete billing information
  • Include farm name and sample descriptions
  • Select appropriate analysis packages
  • Note any special instructions or requirements

3. Package & Ship

Place samples and completed form in a sturdy shipping box. Use sufficient packing material to prevent damage during transit.

  • Use a sturdy box with adequate padding
  • Include the completed submission form
  • Seal the package securely
  • Ship to our laboratory address
